## Domains Excpetions

A Domain Exception is an error that occurs when a business rule or invariant is violated.
Exceptions like, IOexception, SqlException are not domain exceptions, they are infrastructure exceptions.

To define a Domain Exception, we need to certificated that this handles a business rule violation.

So, when a service application cathches a Domain Exception it all ready knows that something wrong about de bussiness
has happened, and it can treat it accordingly translating to a unbiguous message.
